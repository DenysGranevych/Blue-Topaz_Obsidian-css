# Usage

Add the following cssclass directive in YAML

```yaml
---
cssclass: img-captions
---
```


![[obsidian_image.png#left|Figure 1. Hi there|150]]


Then the syntax is like:
`![[xxx.png#position|captions|size]]`
position: left/right
captions: your captions
